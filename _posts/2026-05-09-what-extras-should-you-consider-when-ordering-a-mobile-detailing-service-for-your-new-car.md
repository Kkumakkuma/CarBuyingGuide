## What Extras Should You Consider?
### 1. Interior Fabric Protection
How does interior fabric protection work?  
Interior fabric protection is designed to shield your vehicle’s seat fabric from spills, stains, and general wear and tear. This is especially important if you live in a climate prone to wet weather or if you frequently eat in your car. 

Investing in fabric protection can cost between $50 to $150 depending on the service provider. I once forgot to consider this, and a spilled coffee cost me over $200 for a professional cleaning later!

#### How It Works
- **Application Process**: Typically, the service will spray a protective coating on the seats, which repels liquid and dirt.
- **Duration**: The effects can last up to a year, minimizing your need for frequent cleaning.

#### Service Recommendations
When I called several mobile detailing companies, I found that **DetailXperts** offered excellent fabric protection services for around $120, while **Pinnacle Mobile Detail** priced similar services at $75. The price discrepancy made me realize that it’s essential to compare service quality alongside costs.

### 2. Ceramic Coating
Is ceramic coating worth it in 2026?  
Absolutely, especially if you want to protect the exterior of your car in the long run. Ceramic coating acts as a protective layer, sealing your car’s paint and enhancing its shine. It’s durable enough to withstand UV rays, dirt, and road grime.

#### Cost and Benefits
Expect to pay anywhere from $750 to $1,500 for a professional application. In my research, I discovered that **Ceramic Pro** and **Opti-Coat** are two of the best brands in this field, providing exceptional warranties that can add peace of mind along with their products. 

From my personal experience, I recall spending $900 on a ceramic coating, and three months later, it was still as glossy as day one. This investment can negate the need for frequent waxes and washes.

#### Key Advantages
- **Longevity**: Lasts for several years with proper maintenance.
- **Hydrophobic Properties**: The coating makes water bead up and slide off, reducing dirt accumulation.

### 3. Headlight Restoration Package
What’s the difference between a standard wash and a headlight restoration package?  
While a standard wash covers the exterior, headlight restoration focuses on renewing the clarity of your headlights, which can dull over time due to UV damage. 

A restoration package can range from $30 to $100 and should ideally include thorough cleaning and polishing. I learned this the hard way when I noticed my headlights were foggy after just a few months. A simple, affordable $40 restoration saved me from potentially needing new headlights, which could have cost upwards of $500.

#### Benefits of Restoration
- **Improved Visibility**: Clear headlights enhance nighttime driving safety.
- **Aesthetic Improvement**: They can significantly improve the look of your car.

### 4. Engine Bay Cleaning
How can I ensure a spotless engine bay?  
An often overlooked area, a clean engine bay can help maintain your vehicle’s performance and also make it easier to spot leaks. Some providers include this in premium packages, while others offer it as a separate service typically priced from $75 to $150.

#### Why It Matters
Cleaning your engine bay regularly can prevent corrosion and improve the resale value of your car. I have noticed significant dirt accumulation in my engine bay, which hindered my ability to assess for issues effectively.

#### Service Providers
**Dazzle Mobile Auto Detailing** offers an engine bay cleaning package for around $85, which I found incredibly reasonable compared to other services.
