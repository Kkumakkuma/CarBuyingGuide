## Understanding Fuel Economy Ratings

### What Does Fuel Economy Mean?
Fuel economy ratings are an essential factor for any car buyer today. They measure how efficiently a vehicle uses fuel, typically expressed in miles per gallon (MPG). The higher the MPG figure, the more fuel-efficient the car. 

### Why Fuel Economy Matters
Given that fuel prices can swing wildly—often by over 20% within a few months—having a vehicle with optimal fuel performance can significantly affect your wallet. In fact, with the national average gas price hovering around $3.50 per gallon in 2023, a car that can deliver 50 MPG versus one that merely hits 25 MPG can mean savings of $1,750 annually if you drive 15,000 miles a year.
