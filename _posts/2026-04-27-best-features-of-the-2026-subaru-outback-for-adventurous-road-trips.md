## Fuel Efficiency That Offers More Than Just Numbers

When considering a vehicle for road trips, fuel efficiency can make or break your budget. The 2026 Subaru Outback boasts up to **26 mpg in the city and 33 mpg on the highway**, thanks to its standard 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine. Compared to the **2026 Honda CR-V**, which achieves only **28 mpg combined**, the Outback provides a more efficient choice for highway cruising.

### Real-World Instances

On my road trip, I drove over 600 miles, and my trip computer indicated an average of **30 mpg**. Not only did that stretch my fuel budget, but it also reduced the frequency of pit stops, allowing for a smoother road trip experience.

## Comfort on the Open Road

After spending significant time in the Outback, I found the seat comfort truly exceptional. The spacious interior is perfect for long drives, even for taller passengers like myself, who often sacrifice comfort in smaller vehicles. The seating is designed with long journeys in mind, and the available **leather-trimmed upholstery** adds a touch of luxury.

### Tech Features

- **Infotainment**: The new **11.6-inch touch screen** is a major upgrade, integrating both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto seamlessly. Unlike the **2026 CR-V**, which has a less intuitive system, the Outback’s interface feels user-friendly.
- **Sound System**: If you enjoy music on your journeys, the available **Harman Kardon audio system** elevates the experience significantly.
  
### Storage Versatility

The Outback also shines in terms of cargo space. With its **71.3 cubic feet** when the rear seats are folded down, packing for a road trip becomes effortless. It even has a **roof rack** for additional gear, making it particularly advantageous for those who love outdoor adventures, whether it’s biking or kayaking.

## Safety Features: Driven by Technology

Safety is paramount during road trips, especially with unpredictable weather. The **2026 Subaru Outback** comes standard with the **EyeSight Driver Assist Technology** that features adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and pre-collision braking. These features give you peace of mind, as I experienced while driving through a torrential rainstorm in July; they helped me maintain control and stay safe.

### Additional Safety Highlights

- **Blind-Spot Detection**: Great for highway merging scenarios.
- **Rear Cross-Traffic Alert**: Especially handy for busy parking lots.
- **Standard Airbags**: Comprehensive safety across all models.

According to the **Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS) 2024 ratings**, the Outback was a top safety pick, earning high marks in crash tests.

## Off-Road Capabilities: Beyond the Pavement

### X-Mode Performance

One of my favorite features was the **X-Mode**, which optimizes engine output and braking performance for really challenging conditions.
