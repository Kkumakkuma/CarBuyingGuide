## How Much Do Eco-Friendly Cleaning Supplies for EVs Cost in 2023?

Eco-friendly cleaning supplies for EVs generally range from $10 to $50, depending on the product type and brand. Products might include biodegradable car wash solutions, interior cleaners, and detail kits, which are specifically designed not to damage electric components.

### Why Go Eco-Friendly?
- **Health:** Traditional cleaning supplies can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs).
- **Environmental Impact:** Eco-friendly cleaning reduces harmful chemicals being washed into storm drains.
- **Vehicle Longevity:** Gentle, focused formulations can help protect and preserve your vehicle's finish.

While I was cleaning my Tesla Model 3 last winter, I tried EcoTouch's all-purpose cleaner, priced around $15. Not only was it efficient in removing grime, but it also left my vehicle smelling fresh without harmful chemicals.

## Advanced Tire Pressure Monitoring: The Smart Way to Maintain Range

### The Importance of Smart Tire Pressure Management
While researching accessories, I stumbled upon the benefits of advanced tire pressure monitoring systems (TPMS). These devices, priced between $50 and $150, can sync with your smartphone to alert you about tire health, ensuring optimal performance. A crucial takeaway for me was learning that maintaining proper tire pressure can extend your EV's range significantly. For example, under-inflated tires can reduce efficiency by up to 3%, translating to less mileage per charge.

### My Experience with the OBDLink MX+ TPMS
I purchased the OBDLink MX+ for about $80, which allowed me to monitor tire pressure remotely via my smartphone. On a memorable trip to a nearby mountainous area, I received an alert about a slight tire pressure drop. I was able to stop at a service station before continuing my drive, saving me from potential inefficiency or a flat tire. 

The peace of mind that comes from being informed about tire health cannot be overstated, as it directly impacts safety and range. Especially in an EV where range anxiety can be a concern, having this data at your fingertips is invaluable.

### Keeping Your EV Running Efficiently
Installing a smart TPMS in your EV helps you maintain not only your vehicle’s efficiency but also its safety. As EVs continue to grow in popularity, it's crucial to integrate such smart monitoring systems into your driving routine for enhanced comfort and reliability.
